About Me

I am Omar Rayyan, a passionate full-stack developer from Ramallah, Palestine, with a strong foundation in JavaScript, Python, HTML, CSS, React, Node.js, and more. I graduated from the esteemed Axsos Academy, where I honed my skills in both front-end and back-end technologies.

My love for programming began at a young age, and over the years, I have grown to enjoy every aspect of this job. My perseverance and passion for what I do push me to continually improve and strive for excellence. Whether tackling complex problems or fine-tuning solutions, I always aim to achieve the best possible outcomes in the most efficient way.

I take pride in my problem-solving abilities, efficiency, and hard work. My goal is to create solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations, delivering quality work in the shortest time possible.
